Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first consultation.

What does Dietitian Ekta Matlani specialize in?

Ekta specializes in gut health conditions (IBS, IBD, SIBO, acid reflux, bloating), weight management, metabolic issues (PCOS, thyroid, lipid imbalances), and lifestyle nutrition. She holds an M.Sc in Applied Nutrition from ICMR-NIN and is a Certified Diabetes Educator (IDF).

How much does a nutrition consultation cost?

Ekta offers a free 15-minute discovery call, a 1-month nutrition program at ₹4,000 (includes 4 weekly video calls + meal plan), and a comprehensive 3-month program at ₹10,000 (includes 12 video calls, monthly meal plans, lab report interpretation, and priority WhatsApp support).

Are the consultations available online across India?

Yes, all consultations are conducted fully online via video calls and WhatsApp support, making them accessible to clients anywhere in India.

What are Ekta's qualifications?

Ekta holds an M.Sc in Applied Nutrition from ICMR-NIN (All India Rank 11, NCET 2021), is a Certified Diabetes Educator (IDF), certified in Low FODMAP Diet for IBS (Monash University, Australia), and has expertise in gut-brain axis, microbiome nutrition, and women's hormonal health (PCOS & thyroid).

Will I get a customized meal plan?

Yes. Every program includes a fully personalized meal plan tailored to your health goals, food preferences, lifestyle, and medical history — not a generic template.

How do I get started?

Start with a free 15-minute discovery call — just reach out on WhatsApp or Instagram. There's no obligation, just a genuine conversation about your health goals.
